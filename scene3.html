Predicting 2024 Average Yearly Wage for Connecticut Agencies

For this third scene, you will predict the inflation adjusted wages for 2024 CT State Employees by agency.

Enter the predicted inflation rate as an integer.
For example: Entering 2 is the same as predicting the inflation rate to be 2%.

Connecticut State Employee Wages vs Inflation

This interactive slideshow will investigate wage growth of Connecticut state employees alongside inflation.
Inflation has become a hot topic with its recent rise in the years after the Covid-19 lockdowns.
This first scene will investigate the wages paid in 2023 versus the wages that would have been paid if wages grew inline with inflation.
The four selected state agencies chosen are some of the largest in the state and hovering over them will display the average annual salary.

Select between Real 2023 Wage and Projected 2023 Wage from 2022 Inflation and 2022 Wage

The data for this study comes from CT Data and the State Employee Payroll Data CT Data
The inflation data comes from the Federal Reserve of St. Louis FRED
